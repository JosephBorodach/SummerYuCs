Where we're coming from

We were born and raised on the farm land of Teaneck, fed from its very ground and milked by its very people.


Hello! Yosef Borodach here,

Over the last 20 years while we grew up in Teaneck, the town had just about everything one could want for their family: A strong, upright, but also diverse community, freindly folks, a shul for each child, fantastic sports leagues for kids, and so much more.

So what was missing? From the time I was a young boy, I did so many of the classic old jobs accesible to kids and teens: Shoveling snow, building sukkahs, tutoring, ect. I greatly enjoyed these odd jobs because I was doing them for the wonderful people of my community. And, it was also quite profitable ;)

However, my struggle was always the same: How do I find clients in such a big and competative world? In contrast to the huge platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and Craig's List, where small buisnesses drown, Teaneck Shuls provided me a safe place where my services were not only wanted, but sought for.

And with that, I present to you our community's website: A place where Teaneck Jews can safely unite and communicate, do buisness, and most importantly, perform acts of kindness.

Enjoy!
